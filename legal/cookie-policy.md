## 1. What this covers

This policy explains the cookies and similar local-storage technologies used on
p2ptokens.com and the p2ptokens dashboard. It supplements the
[Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md).

## 2. What are cookies / similar technologies

Cookies are small files stored by your browser. We also use `localStorage` /
`sessionStorage`, which store data on your device similarly. We group them by
purpose below.

## 3. Categories we use

### 3.1 Strictly necessary (always on — no consent required)
These are required for the site/app to function and cannot be switched off.

| Name | Type | Purpose | Retention |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `p2p_age_ack` | localStorage | Records that you confirmed you are 18+ | Until cleared |
| `p2p_cookie_consent` | localStorage | Records your cookie choices | 12 months |
| `p2p_csrf` | cookie | Security / session integrity | Session |

### 3.2 Functional (consent-based)
Remember preferences (e.g., theme, default model). Set only if you consent.

| Name | Type | Purpose | Retention |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `p2p_prefs` | localStorage | UI preferences (e.g., theme, default model) | 12 months |

### 3.3 Analytics (consent-based)
Help us understand usage. Any analytics cookies are **set only after you opt in.**
**We currently set no analytics cookies.**

### 3.4 Advertising
We set **no advertising cookies** and do not use cross-context behavioral
advertising.

## 4. Your choices

- **Consent banner:** on first visit we ask you to accept or reject non-essential
  cookies. Strictly necessary items are always active.
- **Change your mind:** clear the `p2p_cookie_consent` value (or use the
  "Cookie settings" control) to be asked again; you can withdraw consent at any
  time with no effect on prior lawful processing.
- **Browser controls:** you can block or delete cookies in your browser settings;
  blocking strictly necessary items may break functionality.

## 5. Self-hosted instances

Independently operated p2ptokens instances are not controlled by us and may set
their own cookies; this policy covers only the instance operated by
[Legal Entity Name].
