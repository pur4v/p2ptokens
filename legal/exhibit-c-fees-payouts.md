  Operator publishes in marketing will be substantiated and clearly labeled

## 3. Payouts *(planned, v2)*

- **Schedule:** as published in the Provider dashboard when payouts launch (v2).
- **Minimum threshold:** as published in the Provider dashboard when payouts launch (v2).
- Withholding/reversal for failed-verification, fraudulent, or breaching jobs

## 4. Taxes and reporting *(planned, v2)*

- Providers are solely responsible for their taxes.
- The Operator will collect the required tax documentation: **IRS Form W-9** (US
  persons) or the applicable **Form W-8 series** (non-US persons), plus KYC and
  sanctions screening.
- The Operator will report earnings and withhold where legally required, including
  **US Form 1099** reporting and EU **DAC7** platform-operator reporting.
